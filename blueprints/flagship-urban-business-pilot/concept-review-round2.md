# Flagship urban-business pilot — concept gate round 2

Project: `aad722d5-7f48-4e7c-99dd-621a2ff9014c`

Round 1 was rejected because all three openings drifted into crime, fabricated compliance mechanics, implausible starting capital, or institution-scale escalation by chapter 3. Its full artifact remains in `story_flagship_setup_runs.artifact_snapshot`.

## A — Đại Lý Cấp 1: Cuộc Chiến Dòng Tiền

- Engine: payment-term mismatch between retailer, distributor and supplier.
- Opening chain: personal guarantee → margin sacrificed for warehouse acceptance → receivable sold at a discount.
- Strength: clear cash-flow causality and obligations that carry across chapters.
- Risk: three consecutive losses can feel punishing; commercial details around acknowledgement discounts and receivable assignment need domain verification.

## B — Chuỗi Lạnh: Cuộc Đua Nhiệt Độ

- Engine: time and temperature convert operational delay directly into physical spoilage.
- Opening chain: refrigeration incident → product-core evidence at receiving dock → driver dismissal creates capacity shortage.
- Strength: visible ticking clock, professional competence and concrete physical stakes.
- Risk: the fuel-theft driver is still somewhat functional as a villain; the later 500-million deposit must have a documented source before planning.

## C — 48 Giờ Sinh Tử: Hậu Cần Livestream

- Engine: digital order velocity overwhelms physical picking, packing and carrier capacity.
- Opening chain: batch-picking redesign → packaging shortage and volumetric cost → carrier-capacity negotiation drains labor from packing.
- Strength: the most readable opening, naturally escalating state changes, Vietnamese operational texture, and no crime shortcut.
- Risk: the initial 48-hour event alone cannot sustain a long novel; StorySpec must turn the protagonist into a recurring operations-crisis specialist without becoming an episodic reset.

## Recommendation

Choose **C — `concept_hau_can_livestream`**, conditional on StorySpec proving a 20–30 chapter causal arc beyond the first livestream and preserving accumulated clients, liabilities, team relationships and reputation. B is the backup. A needs more domain verification before selection.
